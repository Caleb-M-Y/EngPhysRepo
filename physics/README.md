## Resources

### General
- [HyperPhysics](http://hyperphysics.phy-astr.gsu.edu/hbase/index.html) — Comprehensive concept maps and quick-reference pages for all areas of physics.
- [MIT OpenCourseWare – Physics](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/physics/) — Free lecture notes, exams, and videos for introductory through advanced physics.
- [Khan Academy – Physics](https://www.khanacademy.org/science/physics) — Video lessons on mechanics, E&M, thermodynamics, and more.
- [Physics LibreTexts](https://phys.libretexts.org/) — Free, peer-reviewed open textbooks for undergraduate physics.
- [The Feynman Lectures on Physics](https://www.feynmanlectures.caltech.edu/) — Classic, freely available online from Caltech.

### Problem Solving
- [Physics Forums](https://www.physicsforums.com/) — Community Q&A for homework help and deeper conceptual discussion.
- [Chegg Study](https://www.chegg.com/) *(paid)* — Textbook solutions and tutoring.
