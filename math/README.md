## Resources

### General
- [Paul's Online Math Notes](https://tutorial.math.lamar.edu/) — Excellent free notes and practice problems covering Calculus I–III, Differential Equations, and more.
- [Khan Academy – Math](https://www.khanacademy.org/math) — Video lessons from pre-calc through multivariable calculus and linear algebra.
- [MIT OpenCourseWare – Mathematics](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/mathematics/) — Free lecture notes, exams, and videos from MIT math courses.
- [Professor Leonard (YouTube)](https://www.youtube.com/c/ProfessorLeonard) — Full, clear lecture series for Calculus I–III and Differential Equations.
- [3Blue1Brown – Essence of Calculus (YouTube)](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLZHQObOWTQDMsr9K-rj53DwVRMYO3t5Yr) — Intuition-building visual series on calculus.
- [3Blue1Brown – Essence of Linear Algebra (YouTube)](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLZHQObOWTQDPD3MizzM2xVFitgF8hE_ab) — Geometric intuition for linear algebra.

### Calculus
- [Symbolab](https://www.symbolab.com/) — Step-by-step calculator for derivatives, integrals, series, and more.
- [Wolfram Alpha](https://www.wolframalpha.com/) — Powerful computation and visualization tool.
- [Desmos Graphing Calculator](https://www.desmos.com/calculator) — Interactive 2D graphing.

### Differential Equations
- [Paul's ODE Notes](https://tutorial.math.lamar.edu/Classes/DE/DE.aspx) — Comprehensive free notes.
- [MIT 18.03 Differential Equations](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/18-03-differential-equations-spring-2010/) — Full course materials.

### Linear Algebra
- [MIT 18.06 Linear Algebra (Gilbert Strang)](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/18-06-linear-algebra-spring-2010/) — One of the best linear algebra courses available for free.
